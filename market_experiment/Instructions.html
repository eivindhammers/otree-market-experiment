Welcome to the Market Experiment

In this experiment, you will participate in a market as either a buyer or a seller.

How it works:
  • You will be randomly assigned a role: Buyer or Seller
  • Each participant receives a private value:
    • Buyers receive a private value for the good
    • Sellers receive a private cost to produce the good
  • You will only know your own value/cost, not anyone else's
Trading:
  • Buyers submit a bid: the maximum price they're willing to pay
  • Sellers submit an ask: the minimum price they're willing to accept
  • The market will match buyers and sellers to execute trades
Your Profit:
  • Buyers who trade: Profit = Your Value - Market Price
  • Sellers who trade: Profit = Market Price - Your Cost
  • If you don't trade, your profit is 0
Key insight: Even though no one knows the full picture of supply and demand, the market can still find an efficient outcome. Let's see how!
